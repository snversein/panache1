THE ART OF DIMPLE CREATION

The Art of Dimple Creation

Minimally Invasive Procedure for Natural-Looking Dimples

Dimpleplasty cosmetic procedure designed to create natural-looking dimples that enhance your smile and facial expressions. This minimally invasive surgery creates a small, controlled indentation in the cheek muscle, resulting in charming dimples that appear when you smile.

Our advanced technique ensures natural-looking results that complement your facial anatomy. The procedure is quick, requires minimal recovery time, and produces permanent results.

THE PROCEDURE

The Dimpleplasty Procedure

Precise Marking

Strategic placement marking to ensure perfect positioning and symmetry.

Minimally Invasive Incision

Tiny incision inside the cheek to create a natural-looking indentation.

Muscle Attachment

Creating a small connection between skin and cheek muscle for natural dimple formation.

Precision Suturing

Careful closure with dissolvable sutures for minimal scarring and optimal healing.

Immediate Results

Visible dimples right away, with final appearance developing over 2-3 months.
