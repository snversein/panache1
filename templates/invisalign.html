What Are Aligners?

Aligners are a modern, nearly invisible orthodontic treatment using transparent plastic trays to straighten teeth without the traditional look of metal braces.

Premium Treatment

Invisalign by Align Technology, Inc. is an American-based company providing custom-made, transparent plastic trays that fit snugly over your teeth.

How It Works

Gentle, consistent pressure is applied to move teeth into their ideal positions through a series of custom-made aligners changed every 1-2 weeks.

Are You an Ideal Candidate?

Invisalign Can Help With:
  • Crowded teeth
  • Gaps between teeth
  • Overbite (upper teeth over lower)
  • Underbite (lower teeth over upper)
  • Crossbite
  • Open bite
Good Candidates Should:
  • Be committed to wearing aligners 20-22 hours/day
  • Have all permanent teeth erupted
  • Have good oral health
  • Be willing to change aligners on schedule
  • Attend regular check-up appointments
  • Have realistic expectations

Frequently Asked Questions

All Invisalign products are designed, manufactured and marketed by Align Technology, whose global headquarters are in California, USA, and whose manufacturing operation unit is in Juarez, Mexico.
There are many Invisalign-trained doctors available and listed on this website.
The length of your treatment will depend on your specific needs and will be determined by your Invisalign-trained doctor. The average treatment for an adult is about 12 to 18 months, and for teenagers, treatment length may vary.
You should wear each aligner for 20 to 22 hours a day during your treatment for best results and a timely outcome.
You will visit your Invisalign-trained doctor for a check-up approximately every 6–8 weeks to see how your treatment is progressing.
You should remove your aligners when eating or drinking anything other than water. This prevents staining and damage to the aligners.
You may have a slight lisp initially when you start wearing your aligners, but this typically resolves within a few days as you get used to them.
