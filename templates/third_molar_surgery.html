When is Third Molar Surgery Needed?

  • Impacted Teeth: Teeth that don't have enough room to emerge properly
  • Infection Risk: Partially erupted teeth prone to infection and decay
  • Crowding: Wisdom teeth pushing other teeth out of alignment
  • Cyst Formation: Fluid-filled sacs developing around impacted teeth
  • Pain and Discomfort: Chronic pain or recurring infections
