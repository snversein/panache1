ENHANCE YOUR SMILE

Enhance Your Natural Smile

Advanced Lip Lengthening for Optimal Facial Harmony

Lip lengthening is a specialized cosmetic procedure designed to increase the visible portion of your upper teeth when smiling. This procedure is ideal for individuals with a "gummy smile" or those who feel their upper lip covers too much of their teeth when they smile.

Our surgical technique focuses on creating natural-looking results that enhance your smile while preserving normal lip function and movement.

UNDERSTANDING LIP ANATOMY

Understanding Lip Anatomy

Before Lip Lengthening

Upper lip positioned lower, covering more teeth and potentially showing excessive gum tissue when smiling.

  • Less tooth visibility
  • Gummy smile appearance
  • Asymmetrical smile

After Lip Lengthening

Upper lip repositioned higher, revealing optimal tooth display and creating balanced smile proportions.

  • Ideal tooth visibility
  • Balanced gum display
  • Harmonious smile
THE PROCEDURE

The Lip Lengthening Procedure

Precise Measurements

Detailed analysis of your lip anatomy and smile to determine optimal lift amount.

Strategic Incision

Carefully placed incision along the natural gum line for minimal visibility.

Tissue Repositioning

Gentle lifting and repositioning of lip tissues to reveal more teeth.

Precision Suturing

Fine suturing technique for optimal healing and minimal scarring.

Protective Dressing

Application of protective dressing to support healing and reduce swelling.

IDEAL CANDIDATES

Are You an Ideal Candidate?

Perfect For:

  • Gummy smile (excessive gum show)
  • Short upper lip appearance
  • Desire for more tooth visibility when smiling
  • Good overall oral health
  • Realistic expectations about outcomes

Considerations:

  • Sufficient lip tissue for repositioning
  • No active lip or gum infections
  • Normal lip motor function
  • Understanding of temporary swelling
  • Commitment to post-operative care
