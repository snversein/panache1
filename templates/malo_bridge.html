Innovative Bridge System for Immediate Results

The Malo's Bridge represents a breakthrough in full-arch rehabilitation, offering patients a complete dental restoration solution in just 24-48 hours. This innovative system utilizes strategically placed implants to support a full-arch bridge, restoring both function and aesthetics.

Developed by Dr. Paulo Malo, the Malo's Bridge provides immediate loading capabilities, allowing patients to leave with a beautiful, functional smile on the same day as surgery - perfect for those seeking complete dental transformation without the lengthy healing periods of traditional methods.

HOW IT WORKS

How the Malo's Bridge Works

Strategic Implant Placement

4-6 strategically positioned implants provide optimal support and stability for the entire arch, ensuring long-term success.

Immediate Loading Protocol

Specialized angulation and placement allow for immediate loading, enabling same-day restoration of full dental function.

Custom Bridge Framework

Precision-milled titanium framework provides exceptional strength and ensures accurate fit for optimal comfort and function.

Aesthetic Restoration

High-quality porcelain or zirconia restorations create natural-looking teeth with perfect color matching and translucency.

CANDIDATES

Ideal Candidates for Malo's Bridge

Multiple Missing Teeth

Patients missing most or all teeth in one or both arches seeking complete restoration.

Failed Dentures

THE PROCESS

The Malo's Bridge Procedure

Comprehensive Planning

3D imaging, digital scanning, and precise planning for optimal implant positioning and bridge design.

Surgical Placement

Strategic placement of 4-6 implants under anesthesia, typically completed in 2-3 hours.

Immediate Bridge Loading

Custom-fabricated bridge immediately attached to implants, providing instant function and aesthetics.

Final Integration

Monitoring of osseointegration over 3-6 months with final adjustments and permanent bridge placement.
