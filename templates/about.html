Professional Summary

With over two decades of clinical mastery, Dr. Sandeep Prakash brings a unique blend of experience and innovation to modern dentistry. As a Dentist with 22 years of experience and a specialized Oral & Maxillo-Facial Surgeon with 18 years of expertise, his career has been defined by a commitment to precision and patient-centered care.

Dr. Sandeep Prakash is a highly accomplished Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon with over 22 years of extensive experience in the field of dental surgery. He completed his BDS in 2003 and pursued his specialization in Oral & Maxillo-Facial Surgery, obtaining his MDS degree in 2008.

Throughout his distinguished career of 18 years as an Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon, Dr. Sandeep has developed expertise in a wide range of surgical procedures, from Dental Implantology and Facial Trauma Management to Complex Oral Cancer Surgery and its Reconstruction. He has managed multiple cases of Edentulism by Full Mouth Implants / all on 4/X cases in his clinic with expertise in "Fixed Teeth In a Day".

His approach combines surgical precision with an artistic eye, ensuring optimal functional and aesthetic outcomes for his patients.

He has trained extensively in the field of Minimally Invasive Orthognathic Surgery (MIOS) and Jawline Correction from Barcelona, Spain and Antwerpen, Belgium. These techniques provide sharper Jawline, Slimmer Face, and More Enhanced Facial Features which can truly transform a face.

Dr. Sandeep is also known for his skills in management of TMJ disorders with Facial Asymmetry, use of PSI (Patient Specific Implants) in complex Facial and Temporo-Mandibular Joint reconstruction.

Apart from surgery, Dr. Sandeep also offers a full range of advanced dental treatments. With a strong foundation in both surgical and restorative dentistry, patients receive comprehensive care — from routine procedures to complex dental treatments — all under one roof at Panache Dental.

He stays updated with latest advancements in the field of Dentistry & Maxillo-Facial Surgery by attending numerous International & National Conferences and Hands On Workshops. He also has presented many papers in various State and National Conferences.

He has incorporated many innovative approaches like Digital Dentistry & Minimally Invasive Orthognathic Surgery in his practice. He ensures that even the most challenging cases are handled with seasoned clinical judgment & treatment planning and brings all his experience to shape his results into predictable and high-precision for every patient.
