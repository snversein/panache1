Advanced Buccal Fat Reduction for Facial Harmony

Buccal fat reduction is a sophisticated cosmetic procedure designed to create a more sculpted facial appearance by removing excess fat pads from the cheeks. This procedure targets the buccal fat pads, which can contribute to a round or "chubby" facial appearance.

Our minimally invasive approach ensures natural-looking results that enhance your facial contours without creating an overly hollow or gaunt appearance.

THE PROCEDURE

The Buccal Fat Reduction Process

3D Facial Analysis

Comprehensive assessment of facial structure and fat distribution using advanced imaging.

Precise Marking

Strategic placement marking inside the mouth for optimal fat removal.

Minimally Invasive Removal

Small incision inside the cheek to carefully remove excess buccal fat.

Precision Closure

Dissolvable sutures for seamless healing with no external scars.

Cold Compress Application

Immediate cooling to reduce swelling and promote healing.
