The Foundation of Oral Health

Professional Dental Cleaning for a Healthier Smile

Regular teeth cleaning is the cornerstone of optimal oral health, preventing cavities, gum disease, and maintaining overall wellness. Professional cleanings help remove plaque and tartar buildup that regular brushing cannot address.

Our approach combines advanced technology with gentle techniques to ensure comfort while delivering results that exceed your expectations. Whether you need routine maintenance or targeted treatment, we have the expertise to achieve your goals.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

For optimal oral health, professional cleaning is recommended every 6 months. However, if you have braces, smoke, or have a history of gum disease, your dentist may recommend more frequent visits.
Most patients experience minimal discomfort during professional cleaning. Our gentle techniques and modern tools help ensure a comfortable experience. If you have sensitive teeth, let us know and we can use additional numbing if needed.
After cleaning, avoid eating or drinking staining foods and beverages for a few hours. Maintain good oral hygiene with regular brushing and flossing. Your teeth may feel slightly sensitive briefly, which is normal.
A typical professional cleaning appointment takes 30-45 minutes. More extensive cleaning or if it's been a while since your last visit may take longer. We always take the time needed to ensure thorough cleaning.
