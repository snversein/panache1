The Art of Smile Transformation

Precision Crafted for Natural Beauty

Porcelain veneers are ultra-thin, custom-made shells designed to cover the front surface of teeth, instantly transforming your smile. These dental laminates are crafted from high-quality ceramic materials that mimic the light-reflecting properties of natural tooth enamel.

Whether you're dealing with discoloration, chips, gaps, or misalignment, veneers offer a conservative yet dramatic solution that can correct multiple aesthetic concerns in just a few visits.

OPTIONS

Laminates/Veneer Types

Choosing the Right Type for Your Smile

Porcelain Veneers

Premium ceramic veneers offer superior aesthetics, durability, and stain resistance. Custom-crafted in dental laboratories for perfect fit and natural translucency.

  • Highly stain-resistant
  • Natural light reflection
  • 10-15 year lifespan

Composite Veneers

Direct composite bonding applied chairside for immediate results. More affordable and repairable, perfect for minor corrections and budget-conscious patients.

  • Single visit completion
  • Cost-effective option
  • Easily repairable
  • Lifespan 5-8 years

CANDIDATES

Perfect Candidates for Veneers

Stained or Discolored Teeth

Teeth that don't respond to traditional whitening treatments due to intrinsic staining or medication side effects.

Chipped or Worn Teeth

Restore teeth damaged by grinding, accidents, or general wear and tear to their original appearance.

Gaps Between Teeth

Close unwanted spaces (diastema) between teeth for a more uniform and aesthetically pleasing smile.

Minor Misalignment

Correct slightly crooked or irregularly shaped teeth without the need for extensive orthodontic treatment.

THE PROCESS

The Veneer Process

Consultation & Planning

Comprehensive smile analysis and digital imaging to design your perfect smile transformation.

Minimal Preparation

Gentle removal of 0.3-0.5mm of enamel to create space for the veneer while preserving tooth structure.

Impressions & Design

Digital scans or traditional impressions sent to our dental laboratory for custom veneer fabrication.

Final Bonding

Precise placement and permanent bonding of your custom-crafted veneers for a perfect fit.

AFTERCARE

Caring for Your Veneers

Maintain Good Oral Hygiene

Brush twice daily and floss regularly. Veneers don't decay, but the underlying teeth still need protection.

Avoid Staining Habits

While porcelain is stain-resistant, limit coffee, tea, and red wine to maintain your bright smile.

Wear Protection

Use a night guard if you grind your teeth and wear a mouthguard during sports activities.
