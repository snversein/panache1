REFINEMENT

When Lips Overpower Facial Balance

Refinement Makes the Difference

Lip reduction, also known as cheiloplasty, is a cosmetic surgical procedure designed to reduce the size of the lips, specifically when they appear too large or disproportionate to the rest of the face and create a more balanced and aesthetically pleasing facial appearance.

While some people wish for fuller lips, others may feel that their lips are too thick or bulging, leading to a desire for lip reduction.

BENEFITS

Benefits of Lip Reduction Surgery

Balanced Facial Features

By reducing the size of the lips, the procedure helps restore balance and symmetry to the face, creating a more harmonious appearance.

Boosted Confidence

Many patients experience a boost in self-esteem after the procedure, feeling more comfortable with their appearance.

Improved Facial Proportions

Large lips can overpower other facial features. Lip reduction brings the lips into better proportion with the rest of the face.

Long-Term Results

The results are generally permanent, as the tissue removed does not grow back, allowing patients to enjoy their new lip shape for years.

IDEAL CANDIDATES

Who Is Lip Reduction For?

Naturally Large Lips

Some people are born with lips that are disproportionately large relative to their facial features.

Lip Asymmetry

Can address uneven or asymmetrical lips from natural causes or previous surgeries/injuries.

Previous Enhancements

Those unhappy with lip augmentation results may seek reduction to revert to a natural look.

Congenital Conditions

Conditions like macrocheilia, characterized by abnormally large lips, can be corrected through lip reduction.

THE PROCEDURE

The Procedure at a Glance

Anesthesia

Performed under local anesthesia for patient comfort.

Duration

Downtime

Minimal downtime, patients can return to normal activities quickly.

Sutures

Placed inside the lips, making them not visible externally.
