Meet Dr. Sandeep Prakash

Oral & Maxillo-Facial Surgeon

Dr. Sandeep Prakash is a highly accomplished Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon with over 23 years of extensive experience in the field of dental surgery. He completed his BDS in 2003 and pursued his specialization in Oral & Maxillo-Facial Surgery, obtaining his MDS degree in 2008.

Throughout his distinguished career of 18 years as an OMFS, Dr. Sandeep has developed expertise in a wide range of surgical procedures, from complex facial trauma management to Oral Cancer Surgery & its Reconstruction.

His approach combines surgical precision with an artistic eye, ensuring optimal functional and aesthetic outcomes for his patients.

He has trained extensively in the field of facial aesthetics surgery, orthognathic surgery, and jawline correction which can provide sharper jawline, slimmer face, and more enhanced facial features which can completely transform a face.

Dr. Prakash is also known for his skills in management of TMJ disorders with facial asymmetry, use of PSI (Patient Specific Implants) in complex facial and joint reconstruction & dental implantology. He has managed multiple cases of edentulism by full mouth implants / all on 4/6 cases in his clinic.

He stays updated with latest advancements in the field of dentistry & maxillo-facial surgery by attending numerous international & national conferences and hands on workshops.

He has incorporated many innovative approaches like DIGITAL DENTISTRY & MINIMALLY INVASIVE ORTHOGNATHIC SURGERY / MIOS in his practice.
