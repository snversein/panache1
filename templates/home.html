Professional Summary

Dr. Sandeep Prakash is a highly accomplished Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon with over 17 years of extensive experience in the field of dental surgery and facial reconstruction. He completed his BDS in 2003 and pursued his specialization in Oral & Maxillo-Facial Surgery, obtaining his MDS degree in 2008.

Throughout his distinguished career, Dr. Prakash has developed expertise in a wide range of surgical procedures, from complex facial trauma management to sophisticated esthetic surgeries. His approach combines surgical precision with an artistic eye, ensuring optimal functional and aesthetic outcomes for his patients.

Dr. Prakash is particularly known for his skills in facial esthetic surgery, dental implantology, and maxillofacial trauma care. He stays updated with the latest advancements in surgical techniques and technology, incorporating innovative approaches like digital smile designing and minimally invasive procedures in his practice.
