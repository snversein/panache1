Dr. Sandeep Prakash is a highly accomplished Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon with over 23 years of extensive experience in dental surgery. He completed his BDS in 2003 and pursued specialization in Oral & Maxillo-Facial Surgery, obtaining his MDS degree in 2008.

Throughout his distinguished career, Dr. Sandeep has developed expertise in surgical procedures, from complex facial trauma management to Oral Cancer Surgery & its Reconstruction.

His approach combines surgical precision with an artistic eye, ensuring optimal functional and aesthetic outcomes. He has trained extensively in facial aesthetics surgery, orthognathic surgery, and jawline correction.

He has incorporated innovative approaches like DIGITAL DENTISTRY & MINIMALLY INVASIVE ORTHOGNATHIC SURGERY (MIOS) in his practice.
